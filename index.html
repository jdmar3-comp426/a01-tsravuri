The Festival of Lights...

Learn More

Diwali, or Deepawali, gets its name from the Sanskrit word 'deepavali', which means "row of clay lamps." Diwali is a five-day Festival of Lights, celebrated by millions of Hindus, Sikhs and Jains across the world. Diwali, which for some also coincides with harvest and new year celebrations, is a festival of new beginnings and the triumph of good over evil and light over darkness.
